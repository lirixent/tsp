DAY 53 - GIVE THANKS

Key Scripture/Memory Verse

Ephesians 5:20 (NIV)
"Always giving thanks to God the Father for everything, in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ."

The practice of giving thanks always and for everything is a byproduct of knowledge. When we grasp the knowledge that God is good and loves us unconditionally, a culture of thanksgiving naturally flows from our hearts. Our understanding of God's character and love becomes the foundation of our gratitude.

The verse emphasizes that we give thanks to "God the Father." Our relationship with God transcends mere deity and worshippers; it is one of Father and children. Regardless of our circumstances, our Heavenly Father's posture towards us remains unchanged. His heart is filled with love, and His goodness is exemplified through what He accomplished in Christ for us.

Knowing that our Father's love is unconditional, we can give thanks always and for everything. Our gratitude is not dependent on favorable circumstances; it is an outpouring of our focus on Christ. Regardless of whether we find ourselves in good or challenging situations, we choose to express thanks because our attention is fixed on the unwavering goodness of our Father.

When our focus is on Christ, our gratitude deepens. We recognize the magnitude of God's love demonstrated through the work of Christ. By keeping our eyes on Him, we become more thankful, realizing that our circumstances do not alter the unchanging nature of our Father's love.

Let the knowledge of God's unconditional love and goodness cultivate a heart of gratitude within you. Recognize the Father and children relationship you have with God, and let this understanding shape your thanksgiving. Choose to give thanks always, not based on circumstances, but with your focus on Christ, acknowledging His unchanging love that surpasses all situations.

Reflection

Does God's unconditional love for me prompt me to give thanks?

Confession

I live in a constant state of thanksgiving.
