DAY 4

NEW CREATION

KEY SCRIPTURE/MEMORY VERSE: 2 Corinthians 5:17 (NIV)

“Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creature has come: The old has gone, the new is here!”

The language of scripture is clear – we are not merely renovated or improved versions of our old selves; we are entirely new creatures in Christ. Ephesians 2:10 emphasizes that we are God’s workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works. The transformation goes beyond appearance; it extends to the very core of our being.

Our identity is no longer defined by our old selves, which have passed away. It is rooted in who Christ is and what He has accomplished on our behalf. As new creations, we carry the imprint of God’s craftsmanship, designed for a purpose that aligns with His divine plan.

The word “behold” holds significant meaning in our journey of transformation. It invites us to fix our gaze on the new creature that God has made us to be. Instead of dwelling on the remnants of our old nature – fears, anxieties, and failures – choose to behold the possibilities in Christ.

As you meditate on this truth, behold your possibilities in Christ. See yourself through the lens of freedom, purpose, and the limitless potential that comes with being a new creature. Your identity is not tethered to your past but is anchored to the promises and possibilities found in Christ.

The transformative power lies in becoming what you behold. If you consistently fix your gaze on the freedom, grace, and potential in Christ, your thoughts, actions, and character will align with this new identity. Romans 12:2 (NIV) encourages us to be transformed by the renewing of our minds.

Always remember that you are a new creature in Christ. Behold the possibilities, freedom, and purpose that come with the new identity. Let your thoughts and actions align with the truth of who you are in Christ. The old has gone; the new is here!

REFLECTION

Do I live as a new creation or still struggle with my old identity?

CONFESSION

I walk in purpose and freedom because I am a new creation!
