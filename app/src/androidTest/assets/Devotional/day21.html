DAY 21: WHAT'S YOUR SKIN COLOUR?

Key Scripture/Memory Verse: Ephesians 4:15 AMPC

“Rather, let our lives lovingly express truth [in all things, speaking truly, dealing truly, living truly]. Enfolded in love, let us grow up in every way and in all things into Him Who is the Head, [even] Christ (the Messiah, the Anointed One).”

When God unveils His plan to save mankind from the damning consequences of the Fall through another man (Romans 5:12-17), He makes it foolproof. He ensures that we can see how our new identity in Christ translates into tangible experiences.

Ephesians 2:10 states, "...now that we have been born anew, we are His workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which we are meant to walk in." Every aspect of our lives is subject to the influence of Christ and His Spirit.

Colossians intriguingly expresses this concept in the Message version of Chapter 3:9-14. Verse 14 holds great significance, as it encourages us to clothe ourselves with love, much like an all-purpose or all-season garment. Ponder upon this:

Every time you open your mouth to speak (to yourself or others), let your communication be filled with love. Every time you engage in a transactional or relational interaction with anyone, let love be your guiding principle.

The amplified version suggests that we should put on and envelop ourselves in unselfish love. It can be compared to your skin, the body's largest organ. Consider some of its functions, such as:

  • It serves as a protective covering for organs.
  • It regulates body temperature (similar to how love constrains us, as mentioned in 2 Corinthians 5:14).
  • It reflects your identity.

Therefore, just as one can identify a person's ethnicity by observing their skin, we can perceive your LOVE SKIN and recognize you as a Christian.

Reflection

Do I respond with love?

Confession

I express love at all times, even when it seems impossible to.
