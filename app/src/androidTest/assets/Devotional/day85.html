DAY 85
FOLLOWERS OF GOD

KEY SCRIPTURE/MEMORY VERSE: Ephesians 5:1 (NIV)

"Follow God's example, therefore, as dearly loved children."

In Ephesians 5:1, the apostle Paul provides a profound directive for believers: to follow God's example as dearly loved children. The foundation of our identity as believers is grounded in being children of God. Through faith in Christ, we are adopted into God's family, becoming heirs and co-heirs with Christ (Romans 8:17). This reality transforms our relationship with God from servants to beloved children, recipients of His boundless love and grace.

Ephesians 5:1 challenges us not merely to be recipients of God's love but to imitate Him. To imitate God means to mirror His character, embody His love, and emulate His ways. Matthew 5:48 (NIV) echoes this call: "Be perfect, therefore, as your heavenly Father is perfect." While perfection may seem unattainable, the essence lies in seeking to reflect God's attributes in our daily lives.

Being a child of God comes with responsibilities. As sons and daughters of the King, we are called to represent our Heavenly Father well. Galatians 4:7 (NIV) reminds us, "So you are no longer a slave, but God's child; and since you are his child, God has made you also an heir." Our actions, attitudes, and interactions should align with our identity as children of the Most High.

The responsibility of sonship is best expressed through love. Ephesians 5:2 (NIV) continues, "and walk in the way of love, just as Christ loved us and gave himself up for us as a fragrant offering and sacrifice to God." Love is the guiding principle that should permeate every aspect of our lives. It is selfless, sacrificial, and mirrors the love of our Heavenly Father.
