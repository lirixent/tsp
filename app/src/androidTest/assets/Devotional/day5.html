DAY 5

I AM COMPLETE IN CHRIST

Key Scripture/Memory Verse: Colossians 2:10 (NIV)

“... and in Christ, you have been brought to fullness. He is the head over every power and authority.”

In Christ, believers lack nothing. Despite the challenges and experiences that may leave us feeling deficient, the higher reality is that we are complete in him. This completeness extends beyond our circumstances and is anchored in our identity in Christ.

There are moments when life’s experiences create a sense of deficit or loss. However, as believers, we are called to superimpose the reality of our completeness in Christ over these feelings. Our completeness is not based on external circumstances but on the finished work of Jesus.

Today, you have a choice. You can choose to dwell on the deficits and dysfunctions formed over time, or you can choose the higher reality of your completeness in Christ. Your circumstances may suggest lack, but your identity in Christ declares fullness and perfection.

Consistent reminders of your identity in Christ are crucial. In the face of loss, fears, weakness, or any form of inadequacy, declare your completeness in Him. Your confession shapes your perspective and aligns your thoughts with the truth of God’s Word.

Whether you are facing challenges, uncertainties, or moments of weakness, declare your completeness in Christ. Let your confession be a powerful proclamation that aligns with the authority of Christ, who is the head over every power and authority.

As you navigate the challenges of life, remember that you are complete in Christ. Choose this reality over any sense of lack or deficiency. Declare your completeness in the face of loss, fears, weakness, or any circumstance that may suggest inadequacy. In Christ, you lack nothing; He is the head over every power and authority.

Reflection

In the face of challenges, do I acknowledge my completeness in Christ?

Confession

I am complete in Christ, I lack nothing.
